Creativity and Anxiety¶

The following script includes four steps:

1- Importing the raw data

2- Organizing and cleaning the data (minimal cleaning)

3- Scoring the creativity task

4- Making composite scores for pandemic and baseline anxiety measures

5- Running analyses such as pearson correlation and linear regression
